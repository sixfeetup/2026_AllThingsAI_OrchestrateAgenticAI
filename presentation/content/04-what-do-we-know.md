# What Do We Know About Our Quest?

Tasha has given us the following artifacts in a tarball:

- The buildpack — basically a Dockerfile
- The repo access for their sites
- A database dump
- A file dump of the OGL content

_The clock is ticking. Let's see what we're working with._

???
This is the "quest inventory" moment — what tools and artifacts do we
actually have to work with?

Tasha has handed over a tarball. The buildpack tells us how the apps
are built and deployed. The repos give us the codebase. The database
dump is the state of the world. And the OGL content dump is the thing
that's on fire right now.

Key point: before you throw agents at a problem, you need to understand
the artifacts. Context engineering starts here — what goes into the
agent's context window, in what order, and with what framing.
