# Great, But I Hired You For This Other Thing

_The job is not done — we need to migrate and update their websites._

The OGL crisis is handled. Now the original contract: migrate off
Heroku, modernize the sites, prepare for mobile.

_Time to level up our orchestration._

???
Breather slide. The OGL crisis is handled — the audience has seen agents
do useful work with real artifacts.

But the original contract was about migrating off Heroku and modernizing
the sites. That work is more complex: infrastructure, code changes,
testing, deployment. The stakes are higher and the tasks are less
clear-cut.

This is the pivot from "agents can help in a crisis" to "agents can
handle sustained engineering work." The orchestration needs to level up
from single-agent-with-human to multi-agent coordination.

Transition: and just when you think things are going well... it's a trap.
