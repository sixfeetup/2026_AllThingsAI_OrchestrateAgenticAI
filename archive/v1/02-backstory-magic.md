# The Backstory Magic

Business as usual can feel more like an epic quest into Mordor than a stock photo of smiling generic people gathered around the warm light of a laptop.

- One second you're planning a cheery offsite
- The next it's very dark and you might be eaten by a grue
- Whether good news or bad, adapting quickly requires tools and experience

> "We become what we behold. We shape our tools and then our tools shape us."
> — Marshall McLuhan
