# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT (SYNTHETIC - DEMO ONLY)

**Project Code:** OPERATION: DUNGEON CRAWL **Date:** March 5, 2026

## 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Artificer (Contractor) shall provide specialized agentic orchestration
services to the Guild (Client) as defined in Exhibit A. Services will be
delivered via API and authenticated through the Model Context Protocol (MCP).

## 2. FEES AND PAYMENT
**2.1 Service Fees:** Client shall pay Contractor the sum of $50,000 upon
execution. **2.2 Payment Terms:** Net 30 days. Late payments shall accrue
interest at 1.5% per month.

## 3. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
**3.1 Limitation of Liability:** Contractor's total liability for any claim
arising out of this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client in
the preceding six (6) months. **TRAP #1 (The Tricky Clause):** Notwithstanding
Section 3.1, Contractor’s liability for "Data Integrity Errors caused by
Autonomous Loops" shall be unlimited and shall include consequential damages,
including loss of potential future revenue.

## 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All custom "Skills" and "CLAUDE.md" files developed specifically for the Guild
shall be considered Works Made For Hire and owned by the Guild.

## 5. TERM AND TERMINATION
**5.1 Term:** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue
for twelve (12) months. **TRAP #2 (The Auto-Renewal):** This Agreement shall
automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either party
provides written notice of non-renewal at least 180 days prior to the end of the
then-current term. **TRAP #3 (The Missing Element):** *Note to Auditor: There is
no "Termination for Convenience" clause in this section. If the Guild wants to
leave early, they are locked into the full 12-month term.*

## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other party's "Prompts,"
"System Instructions," and "Training Data."

## 7. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
**7.1 Uptime:** Contractor guarantees 99.9% uptime for the Warforged API. **7.2
Credits:** If uptime falls below 99.0%, Client is entitled to a 5% credit on the
next month's bill. **TRAP #4 (The Contradiction):** See Section 10.4 below.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of North Carolina.

## 10. MISCELLANEOUS
**10.4 Force Majeure:** Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by
"Solar Flares," "GPU Shortages," or "Hallucination Cascades." **TRAP #4 (The
Contradiction):** In the event of a Force Majeure event as defined in 10.4,
Contractor’s SLA obligations in Section 7.1 are waived, and no credits shall be
issued, notwithstanding any language in Section 7.2 to the contrary.
